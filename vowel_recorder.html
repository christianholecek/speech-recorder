Language Recorder

Participant Information & Consent

Thank you very much for participating!
Before you start, please read the information and instructions carefully.

What you will do: You will read short sentences out loud and record them. The session takes about 20 minutes.

Tasks and break: You may complete the two tasks in any order (Task A first or Task B first). If you complete both tasks in one session, please take a break of at least 15 minutes between them.

Before you start (important):

  • Please use a laptop/desktop computer (not a phone or tablet).
  • Ideally, be alone in a quiet room (close windows, silence notifications).
  • Sit close to your microphone (about 30–50 cm) and speak clearly at a normal volume.
  • For each recording: press Start recording, wait one second, then speak. After you finish, wait one second, then press Stop.

Microphone permission: Your browser will ask for permission to access your microphone. Please click Allow. On the microphone setup page, you can make a test recording to check your microphone. Test recordings are not saved.

Saving your recordings: After each sentence, click Accept & save. Your recording will be uploaded. Once the upload is complete, you can proceed to the next sentence.

Data: Audio recordings and questionnaire responses will be stored securely and used for research purposes. Data will be anonymised for publication.

Voluntary: Participation is voluntary. You can withdraw at any time before submission.

Contact: If anything is unclear or you experience technical problems, please contact Christian Holecek (christian.holecek@edu.uni-graz.at), the student assistant who sent you this link.
